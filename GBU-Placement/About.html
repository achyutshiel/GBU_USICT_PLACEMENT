About USCIT

The ICT Placement Cell of Gautam Buddha University (GBU) is dedicated to advising and counseling students on career options. The ICT Placement Cell serves as a primary resource for students to branch out from their classrooms and explore and apply their classroom learning. By staying updated with the latest trends and providing current information, the ICT Placement Cell works towards finding the best opportunities for students, including summer training/internships, live projects, national and international events, and employment vacancies. To help students compete in todayâ€™s competitive environment, the ICT Placement Cell offers individual counseling on a one-to-one basis, arranges seminars and group sessions focused on career choices, and enhances students' interview and group discussion skills. Additionally, it assists with resume preparation and job application drafts. The Manager of ICT Placement and the placement team maintain ongoing communication with various academic institutions and corporate establishments. The cell operates year-round to generate placement and training opportunities for students, acting as an interface between students, faculty, and the corporate world. The ICT Placement Cell engages in continuous interaction with the industry, bringing industry experiences to the campus and understanding industry needs and requirements.
The ICT Placement Cell has a focused mandate to progressively enhance the synergies between the University and the Industry.
