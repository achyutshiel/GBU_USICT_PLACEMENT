About USCIT

Established in 2009, the University School of Information and Communication Technology (USICT) has evolved into two distinct departments: Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE). USICT is renowned for producing skilled professionalsâ€”doctoral, postgraduate, and undergraduateâ€”who excel in the dynamic fields of Information and Communication Technology (ICT).

Our commitment to excellence in research and education is reflected in our year-round engagement with distinguished alumni, academics, and industry professionals. As ICT continues to influence nearly every aspect of modern society, its significance for our nation's technological infrastructure is undeniable.

We take pride in the accomplishments of our alumni, who include award-winning researchers and leaders at global organizations such as Siemens, Qualcomm, Nokia, and HCL, among others.

USICT offers a variety of programs, including full-time and part-time doctoral degrees, as well as comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs in CSE and ECE, along with integrated degree options. Our facilities include spacious classrooms equipped with state-of-the-art teaching aids, such as LCD panels and an advanced learning management system.

Our faculty members are highly qualified and dedicated to both teaching and cutting-edge research in areas such as Software Engineering, Data Science, Wireless Communication, Artificial Intelligence, VLSI Design, Machine Learning, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Big Data Analytics. Many faculty members are recognized for their contributions through national and international awards and actively participate in editorial roles for prestigious journals, including IEEE and ACM.

These are exciting times for ICT students and researchers as the field contributes to innovative tools and techniques across various sectors.

At USICT, we focus on equipping students to tackle growing technological and societal challenges through self-discipline, hard work, and holistic personality development. We foster a spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship, preparing our students not only to be job seekers but also job creators. Our curriculum is designed to be flexible and interdisciplinary, enhancing our students' readiness for the industry.

Prof. Sanjay Kumar Sharma
